Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TrackPro

What is TrackPro GPS Tracking System?

TrackPro is a comprehensive GPS tracking platform that allows you to monitor vehicles, assets, and people in real-time. Our system combines cutting-edge GPS technology with AI-powered analytics to provide accurate location tracking, geofencing, route history, and intelligent alerts.

How accurate is the GPS tracking?

TrackPro provides industry-leading GPS accuracy within 5-10 meters (16-33 feet) in optimal conditions. Accuracy can be affected by factors such as tall buildings, dense forests, or tunnels. Our system uses multi-satellite technology (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo) to ensure the best possible accuracy.

Do I need special hardware to use TrackPro?

Yes, you'll need a GPS tracking device compatible with TrackPro. We offer various devices for different use cases: vehicle trackers, personal trackers, and asset trackers. You can purchase devices from our online store or use compatible third-party devices. Contact our sales team for device recommendations.

How often does the location update?

TrackPro updates location data in real-time with intervals ranging from 1 second to 1 minute, depending on your plan and settings. Pro and Enterprise users can set update intervals as low as every 1 second for critical tracking needs. You can adjust the update frequency in your device settings.

What is geofencing and how does it work?

Geofencing creates virtual boundaries around specific locations. When a tracked device enters or exits a geofenced area, you receive instant notifications via push notification, email, or SMS. You can create unlimited geofences with custom shapes (circle or polygon) and set different alert rules for each zone.

Can I track multiple devices simultaneously?

Absolutely! TrackPro supports multiple device tracking on all plans. Basic plan includes 1 device, Pro plan supports up to 5 devices, and Enterprise plan offers unlimited devices. All devices are displayed on a single map dashboard with color-coded markers for easy identification.

Does TrackPro work internationally?

Yes! TrackPro works in over 150 countries worldwide. Our GPS devices use global cellular networks and satellite systems, ensuring reliable tracking wherever your assets travel. International roaming rates may apply depending on your device and cellular plan.

How long does the device battery last?

Battery life varies by device model and usage patterns. Typically, our devices last 7-30 days on a single charge. Vehicle trackers connected to car power can operate indefinitely. Personal trackers average 10-14 days. You'll receive low battery alerts when the charge drops below 20%.

How do I install a GPS tracker in my vehicle?

Installation depends on the device type. Plug-and-play OBD-II trackers simply connect to your vehicle's OBD port (usually under the steering wheel). Hardwired trackers require professional installation. Personal trackers need no installation. Each device comes with detailed instructions, and our support team is available 24/7 to help.

Are the GPS trackers waterproof?

Most of our trackers have IP67 water resistance rating, meaning they can withstand rain, splashes, and brief submersion up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. For marine or extreme weather applications, we offer IP68-rated devices with enhanced waterproofing. Check individual product specifications for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency for Enterprise plans. All payments are securely processed through industry-leading payment processors with PCI DSS compliance.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees or penalties. If you cancel, you'll retain access to your account until the end of your current billing period. You can reactivate your subscription anytime, and all your historical data will be preserved.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with TrackPro within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund. Hardware purchases may have different return policies - please check our Returns Policy for details.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. TrackPro uses bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission. Your location data is stored on secure, redundant servers with multiple backups. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Our platform is GDPR compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified.

Does TrackPro have a mobile app?

Yes! TrackPro offers native mobile apps for iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. The apps provide full functionality including real-time tracking, alerts, geofencing, and route playback. Download from the App Store or Google Play Store. The mobile apps sync seamlessly with the web dashboard.

Can I integrate TrackPro with my existing software?

Yes! TrackPro provides a comprehensive REST API for integration with your applications. We offer SDKs for JavaScript, Python, PHP, and Java. Enterprise customers can also use webhooks for real-time event notifications. Full API documentation is available in your dashboard.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

GPS devices store location data locally when cellular connection is lost. Once connection is restored, all buffered data is automatically uploaded to your account. This ensures no tracking data is lost during connectivity gaps. Devices can typically store 7-14 days of offline data.

How do I export my tracking data?

You can export your tracking data in multiple formats including CSV, Excel, JSON, and PDF. Go to Settings > Advanced > Data Export and select your preferred format and date range. Enterprise users can also schedule automatic exports via API or webhook integration.
